## DESIGN NOTES

- **Background**: Near-black (#0A0A0A) with subtle gradient
- **Typography**: Clean, large headlines. Max 2 font weights.
- **Animations**: Minimal. Fade-in on scroll only. No bouncing, no spinning.
- **Mobile-first**: Everything readable on iPhone without zooming
- **CTA color**: High contrast — white or bright accent on dark
- **Spacing**: Generous. Let each section breathe.
- **Total height**: ~4-5 mobile screens max (not 15 like current)
